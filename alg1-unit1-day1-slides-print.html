Keywords are Clues!

ADDITION (+)

sum, plus, more than, increased by

SUBTRACTION (-)

difference, minus, less than, decreased by

MULTIPLICATION (ร)

product, times, of, twice

DIVISION (รท)

Slide 5

Watch Out! Turn-Around Words

Phrases with "than" reverse the order.

"10 less than a number n"

n - 10

"8 more than a number y"

y + 8
