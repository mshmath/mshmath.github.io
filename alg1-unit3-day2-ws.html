Worked-Out Examples

Example 1: Simplify then Add

\( \sqrt{12} + \sqrt{75} \)

\(\rightarrow \sqrt{4 \cdot 3} + \sqrt{25 \cdot 3}\)

\(\rightarrow 2\sqrt{3} + 5\sqrt{3}\)

\(\rightarrow 7\sqrt{3}\)

Example 2: Multiply then Simplify

\( 2\sqrt{5} \cdot 3\sqrt{10} \)

\(\rightarrow (2 \cdot 3)\sqrt{5 \cdot 10}\)

\(\rightarrow 6\sqrt{50}\)

\(\rightarrow 6\sqrt{25 \cdot 2} \rightarrow 6 \cdot 5\sqrt{2}\)

\(\rightarrow 30\sqrt{2}\)
