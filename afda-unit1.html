AFDA Unit 1: Function Families

Virginia Standards of Learning (SOL)

AFDA.1:

The student will investigate and analyze function families and their characteristics. Key concepts include domain and range, intercepts, zeros, intervals of increase and decrease, asymptotes, and end behavior.

AFDA.2:

The student will use knowledge of transformations to analyze, describe, and graph functions. This includes translations (shifting), reflections (flipping), and dilations (stretching or shrinking).
