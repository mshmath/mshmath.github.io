The Modeling Matrix
Family Shape Pattern (Logic) Keywords
Linear $y = mx + b$ Straight Line Constant Rate
Adds/Subtracts the same amount each step. 		Per Every Constant Rate Flat Fee
Quadratic $y = ax^2+...$ Parabola (U) Symmetry
Goes Up then Down (or vice versa). Has a Peak. 		Projectile Thrown Max/Min Area Gravity
Exponential $y = a(b)^x$ J-Curve Rapid Change
Multiplies by a factor. Starts slow, ends fast. 		Doubles Halves % Growth Population Bacteria
Logarithmic $y = \log(x)$ r-Shape Plateau
Grows fast at first, then levels off slowly. 		Decibels Richter pH Scale Magnitude
Key Distinctions
  • Linear vs Exp: Linear adds repeatedly ($+$). Exponential multiplies repeatedly ($\times$).
  • Quad vs Exp: Quadratic comes back down (symmetry). Exponential goes up forever (or approaches 0).
Real World Domains
In modeling, $x$ (Time/Distance) usually cannot be negative.
Domain Restriction: $x \ge 0$.

Modeling Drill

Levels 3-4: Application

Level 3: Solving in Context
Use the given model to solve the problem. Show basic work.
19.
Linear: A taxi charges $3.00 plus $2.00 per mile. Model: $C = 2m + 3$.
How much does a 10-mile ride cost?
20.
Exponential: A bacteria culture starts with 100 and doubles every hour. Model: $P = 100(2)^t$.
How many bacteria after 3 hours?
21.
Quadratic: An object's height is $h = -16t^2 + 64t$.
Find the height at $t = 1$ second.
22.
Linear: You have $500 and spend $50 per week. Model: $B = 500 - 50w$.
How much is left after 4 weeks?
23.
Logarithmic: Sound level is $L = 10 \log(I)$. If Intensity $I = 100$, find $L$.
(Note: $\log(100) = 2$)
24.
Quadratic: A ball dropped from 100 ft follows $h(t) = -16t^2 + 100$.
When does it hit the ground ($h=0$)? (Solve $16t^2 = 100$)
Level 4: Advanced Analysis
Interpret meanings of constants and variables.
25.
In the linear model $C = 15h + 75$ (Cost of repair), what does the 75 represent in the real world?
26.
In the quadratic path $h = -16(t-2)^2 + 100$, what does the 100 represent?
27.
In the exponential model $V = 20000(0.85)^t$ for car value, is the car gaining or losing value? How do you know?
28.
Why is the domain for a "Time vs Distance" graph usually $x \ge 0$?
29.
In the model $y = 300(1.05)^x$, what does the 1.05 tell us about the percentage growth?
30.
A tank holds 1000 gallons and drains at 50 gal/min ($y = -50x + 1000$). What is the real-world meaning of the x-intercept?
