Today's Objectives

  • Identify keywords for math operations.
  • Translate verbal phrases into algebraic expressions.
  • Use a variable to represent an unknown quantity.

Warm-Up: Numerical Expressions

Write a numerical expression for each phrase:

  1. The sum of 8 and 5.
  2. The product of 10 and 3.
  3. 12 less than 20.

Keywords are Clues!

ADDITION (+)

sum, plus, more than, increased by

SUBTRACTION (-)

difference, minus, less than, decreased by

MULTIPLICATION (ร)

product, times, of, twice

DIVISION (รท)

quotient, divided by, ratio of

Watch Out! Turn-Around Words

Phrases with "than" reverse the order.

"10 less than a number n"

n - 10

"8 more than a number y"

y + 8
