  • Term: Parts separated by + or - signs.
  • Degree: The highest exponent.
  • Leading Coefficient: The number in front of the term with the highest degree.
  • Constant: A number with no variable.

Adding (+)
Just combine Like Terms.
(Same Variable, Same Exponent)

$2x + 5x = \mathbf{7x}$
Subtracting (-)
1. Distribute the Negative Sign to the entire second group.
2. Combine Like Terms.
