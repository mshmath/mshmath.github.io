My Examples

Here, I will be inputting my own worked-out examples to illustrate the concepts from the lessons.

The examples will use MathJax to render mathematical notation clearly, like this sample of the quadratic formula:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$$

When the value of $a$ is not zero, the formula provides the roots of the quadratic equation.
