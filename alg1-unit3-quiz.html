Unit 3 Quiz: Radical Expressions

1. What is \( \sqrt{72} \) in simplest radical form?

  1. \( 2\sqrt{18} \)
  2. \( 3\sqrt{8} \)
  3. \( 6\sqrt{2} \)
  4. \( 36\sqrt{2} \)

2. What is \( \sqrt[3]{54} \) in simplest radical form?

  1. \( 3\sqrt[3]{2} \)
  2. \( 2\sqrt[3]{3} \)
  3. \( 3\sqrt[3]{6} \)
  4. \( 9\sqrt[3]{6} \)

3. What is the sum of \( 5\sqrt{7} + 2\sqrt{7} \)?

  1. \( 7\sqrt{14} \)
  2. \( 7\sqrt{7} \)
  3. \( 10\sqrt{7} \)
  4. \( 3\sqrt{7} \)

4. Simplify the expression: \( \sqrt{50} - \sqrt{8} \)

  1. \( \sqrt{42} \)
  2. \( 7\sqrt{2} \)
  3. \( 3\sqrt{2} \)
  4. \( 21 \)

5. What is the product of \( \sqrt{6} \cdot \sqrt{8} \)?

  1. \( \sqrt{14} \)
  2. \( 2\sqrt{12} \)
  3. \( 8\sqrt{3} \)
  4. \( 4\sqrt{3} \)

6. Which of the following is equivalent to \( x^{\frac{3}{4}} \)?

  1. \( \sqrt[3]{x^4} \)
  2. \( \frac{3x}{4} \)
  3. \( \sqrt[4]{x^3} \)
  4. \( x\sqrt[4]{x^3} \)

7. What is \( 27^{\frac{2}{3}} \)?

  1. 18
  2. 3
  3. 9
  4. \( \sqrt{27^2} \)

8. Rationalize the denominator: \( \frac{5}{\sqrt{3}} \)

  1. \( \frac{5\sqrt{3}}{3} \)
  2. \( \frac{\sqrt{15}}{3} \)
  3. \( 5\sqrt{3} \)
  4. \( \frac{5\sqrt{3}}{9} \)

9. Simplify the expression completely: \( \frac{6}{\sqrt{3}} \)

  1. \( \frac{\sqrt{18}}{3} \)
  2. \( 2\sqrt{3} \)
  3. \( \frac{6\sqrt{3}}{9} \)
  4. \( 3\sqrt{2} \)

10. Simplify the expression: \( 3\sqrt{2}(\sqrt{6} + \sqrt{2}) \)

  1. \( 3\sqrt{12} + 3\sqrt{4} \)
  2. \( 18\sqrt{3} + 6 \)
  3. \( 6\sqrt{3} + 6 \)
  4. \( 9\sqrt{2} \)
