Analyze This:
Is the reverse true?

Warm Up Topic 2
Logic Puzzle:
Which two statements mean the same thing?
A) If it rains, I get wet.
B) If I get wet, it rains.
C) If I don't get wet, it didn't rain.
Contrapositive is Equivalent!
