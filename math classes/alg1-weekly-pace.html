Algebra 1: 16-Week Pacing Guide

Welcome to your Algebra 1 course! This guide outlines our comprehensive 16-week plan, detailing what we'll cover each day from Monday to Thursday. Fridays are dedicated to personalized support during office hours. Our goal is to build a strong understanding of algebraic concepts, prepare you for the SOL exam, and boost your math confidence.

Important Notes:

  • LMS: We will be using Synergy Gradebook as our Learning Management System. This is where you'll find grades and official course communications.
  • Interactive Units: For each unit, we will utilize interactive notes and practice problems to guide our learning. These are designed for active engagement.
  • In-Class Practice: We will regularly work on problems from IXL and previous released SOL test materials during class to solidify understanding and prepare you for the exam.
  • Quizzes: A unit quiz will be given on the last day (Thursday) of each unit to check your progress.
  • Final Exams (Mock SOL Practice):
    • Part A Final Exam (Week 8): This will be a practice SOL test covering Units 1-6.
    • Part B Final Exam (Week 16): This will also be a practice SOL test covering Units 7-14.
    • These exams are designed to give you experience with the SOL format and help prepare you for the official state exam.
  • Flexibility: While this guide provides a clear structure, please note that it is subject to minor adjustments based on class progress and needs.
  • Support: Remember to utilize Friday office hours (10 AM - 12 PM) for any additional support you need, including tutoring, extra help, or make-up tests/assignments.
