Section 1: What a Fraction Represents

For each description, write the fraction.

  1. A pizza has 8 slices and you eat 3. What fraction did you eat? $\frac{3}{8}$
  2. A bag has 10 marbles. 7 are red. What fraction of the marbles are red? $\frac{7}{10}$
  3. There are 5 birds on a wire. 2 fly away. What fraction of the birds flew away? $\frac{2}{5}$
  4. You have 12 pencils and 5 are sharpened. What fraction is sharpened? $\frac{5}{12}$
  5. A recipe needs 4 cups of flour. You only have 1 cup. What fraction of the flour do you have? $\frac{1}{4}$
  6. There are 20 students in a class. 11 are girls. What fraction of the class are boys? $\frac{9}{20}$
