Percent \(\leftrightarrow\) Decimal

This is the easiest conversion! It's all about moving the decimal point two places.

Decimal \(\rightarrow\) Percent

Move Right \(\rightarrow\)

0.25 \(\rightarrow\) 25%

Percent \(\rightarrow\) Decimal

Move Left \(\leftarrow\)

45% \(\rightarrow\) 0.45

Fraction \(\rightarrow\) Decimal

A fraction is just a division problem in disguise!

To convert a fraction to a decimal, divide the numerator by the denominator.

\(\frac{3}{4} = 3 \div 4 = 0.75\)

Remember: "Top dog in the house!" The top number goes inside the division box.

Decimal \(\rightarrow\) Fraction

Use the "Say It, Write It, Simplify It" method.

0.8

1. Say It: "Eight tenths"

2. Write It: \(\frac{8}{10}\)

3. Simplify It: \(\frac{8 \div 2}{10 \div 2} = \frac{4}{5}\)

Percent \(\rightarrow\) Fraction

Remember, percent means "out of 100".

Just put the number over 100 and simplify!

40% = \(\frac{40}{100} = \frac{4}{10} = \frac{2}{5}\)

I Do: Putting it all Together

Let's convert \(\frac{1}{5}\) to a decimal and a percent.

To a Decimal:

Divide: \(1 \div 5 = 0.2\)

To a Percent:

Move decimal 2 places right:

0.2 \(\rightarrow\) 0.20 \(\rightarrow\) 20%

\(\frac{1}{5} = 0.2 = 20\%\)
