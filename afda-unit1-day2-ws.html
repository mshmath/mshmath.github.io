Worked Example

Describe the translations for the equation $f(x) = (x-3)^2 + 4$

  1. Horizontal Shift: The $(x-3)$ is inside the parentheses. Since it's the opposite of the sign, this is a shift right 3 units.
  2. Vertical Shift: The $+4$ at the end is outside the function, which means a shift up 4 units.
