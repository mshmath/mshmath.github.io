1. How would you classify the polynomial \(7x^3 - 2x + 5\) by the number of its terms?

A. Monomial

B. Binomial

C. Trinomial

D. Constant

2. What is the result of adding \((4x^2 - 3x + 1)\) and \((x^2 + 5x - 6)\)?

A. \(5x^2 + 2x - 5\)

B. \(4x^2 + 2x - 5\)

C. \(5x^2 - 8x - 7\)

D. \(5x^4 + 2x^2 - 5\)

3. Simplify the expression: \((8y^2 + 5y - 2) - (3y^2 - 2y + 1)\).

A. \(5y^2 + 3y - 1\)

B. \(11y^2 + 3y - 1\)

C. \(5y^2 + 7y - 3\)

D. \(5y^2 + 7y - 1\)

4. What is the product of \((x + 4)\) and \((x + 5)\)?

A. \(x^2 + 20\)

B. \(x^2 + 9x + 20\)

C. \(2x + 9\)

D. \(x^2 + x + 20\)

5. What is the result of multiplying \(2x\) and \((x^2 - 4x + 3)\)?

A. \(2x^3 - 8x^2 + 6x\)

B. \(2x^2 - 8x + 6\)

C. \(2x^3 - 8x^2 + 3\)

D. \(x^2 - 2x + 3\)

6. What is the greatest common factor (GCF) of the terms in the expression \(8x^3 + 12x\)?

A. 4

B. x

C. \(4x\)

D. \(8x^3\)

7. Which of the following is the correct factorization of the trinomial \(x^2 + 6x + 8\)?

A. \((x + 6)(x + 8)\)

B. \((x - 4)(x - 2)\)

C. \((x + 4)(x + 2)\)

D. \((x + 8)(x - 2)\)

8. Which expression is equivalent to \(x^2 - 81\)?

A. \((x - 9)(x - 9)\)

B. \((x - 9)(x + 9)\)

C. \((x + 9)(x + 9)\)

D. \((x - 81)(x + 1)\)

9. What is the result of dividing \((12x^4 - 6x^2)\) by \(3x\)?

A. \(4x^3 - 2x\)

B. \(4x^4 - 2x^2\)

C. \(9x^3 - 3x\)

D. \(4x^3 - 2\)

10. Which polynomial is written in standard form?

A. \(8x + 3x^2 - x^3 + 4\)

B. \(4 - x^3 + 3x^2 + 8x\)

C. \(-x^3 + 3x^2 + 8x + 4\)

D. \(3x^2 + 8x - x^3 + 4\)
