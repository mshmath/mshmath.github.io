3. Which law allows you to conclude: "If I study, then I will be happy" from "If I study, I'll pass" and "If I pass, I'll be happy"?

4. The conditional statement $p \rightarrow q$ is only false when...

9. In a class of 50 students, 28 play basketball, 22 play soccer, and 10 play both. How many students play neither sport?
