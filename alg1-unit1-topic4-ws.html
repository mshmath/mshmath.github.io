5x + 7
COEFFICIENT The number in front of a variable (5).
VARIABLE The letter representing an unknown (x).
CONSTANT A number by itself with no variable (7).
TERM Parts separated by + or - signs.
The "Sign" Rule:
The sign (+ or −) always belongs to the term directly after it.
In 5x − 3y, the 3y is negative.
Concept 1: Combining Like Terms
Like Terms have...
  • The SAME Variable
  • The SAME Exponent
3x and -5x
2x² and 3x
How to Combine
1. Add or subtract the Coefficients.
2. Keep the variable EXACTLY the same.
4a + 3a = 7a
Concept 2: Distributive Property
a(b + c) = ab + ac
Multiply the term on the outside by EVERY term on the inside.
2(x + 4) → 2(x) + 2(4) → 2x + 8
