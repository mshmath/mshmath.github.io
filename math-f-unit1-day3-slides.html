The Big Idea: Inverse Operations

"Inverse" is a fancy word for opposite.

Inverse operations are operations that "undo" each other. They are partners.

Addition (+)

is the inverse of

Subtraction (-)

Multiplication (x)

is the inverse of

Division (÷)

What is a Fact Family?

A fact family is a group of four math facts that all use the same three numbers. It shows the inverse relationship between multiplication and division.

The Fact Family for 3, 4, 12

Multiplication:

3 x 4 = 12

Multiplication:

4 x 3 = 12

Division:

12 ÷ 3 = 4

Division:

12 ÷ 4 = 3

Notice how division always starts with the biggest number!
