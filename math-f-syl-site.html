Course Overview

Welcome to Math Foundations! This course is designed to help you build a strong and confident understanding of the essential pre-algebra skills needed to succeed in Algebra 1. The course is delivered through a self-paced online program, allowing you to learn at a pace that works best for you.

My role in this class is to be your guide and support system. While the primary lessons and assignments are provided by the online program, I am here to proctor, offer one-on-one help, provide examples, and lead mini-lessons whenever you need clarification or extra practice on a topic.


Materials Needed

  • Notebook or binder with paper for notes and practice problems
  • Pencils and erasers
  • Calculator (scientific or Desmos online calculator recommended)
  • Access to a laptop or computer with a reliable internet connection

Units & Topics Covered

All units are aligned with the Virginia Standards of Learning (SOL) for pre-algebra to prepare you for Algebra I.

  • Unit 1: The Real Number System
  • Unit 2: Algebraic Expressions
  • Unit 3: Solving Equations
  • Unit 4: Solving Inequalities
  • Unit 5: Ratios, Proportions & Percentages
  • Unit 7: Introduction to Functions
  • Unit 8: Basic Geometry Concepts

Course Completion & Progress

Your progress is determined by your completion of the modules within our online learning program. Successful completion is based on finishing all required lessons, practice problems, and assessments in the online system. It is important to work consistently to stay on track.


Expectations for Success

  1. Work Consistently: Dedicate regular time each week to the online modules.
  2. Ask for Help: Don't hesitate to reach out to me when you're stuck. My job is to help you succeed!
  3. Stay Engaged: Take notes as you go through the online lessons.
  4. Use Your Resources: Make full use of the class resource page.
  5. Try Your Best: A positive attitude is key to success.
