Course Roadmap

Our 8-week journey is broken down into seven core units. This roadmap outlines the key topics we will explore each step of the way.

Unit 1: Reasoning, Logic & Venn Diagrams

Understanding logical reasoning and its application in geometry.

Unit 2: Lines, Angles, and Parallelism

Properties of lines, angles, and parallel lines, including transversal relationships.

Unit 3: Transformations & Symmetry

Exploring translations, reflections, rotations, dilations, and various types of symmetry.

Unit 4: Triangles: Properties & Congruence

Classifying triangles, understanding their properties, and proving congruence.

Unit 5: Similarity & Trigonometry

Concepts of similar figures and an introduction to right-triangle trigonometry.

Unit 6: Polygons & Circles

Properties of various polygons, circumference, area, and equations of circles.

Unit 7: 3D Figures

Calculating volume, surface area, and analyzing effects of dimensional change.
