Your Ultimate Guide to Free Math Tools Online

Graphing & Solving Tools

Use these sites to solve and graph equations. The Desmos calculator can be used on the SOL Exam.

Desmos Graphing Calculator

What it is: The best online graphing calculator, hands down. Just type in an equation and it shows you the graph instantly. You can plot points, make tables, and play with sliders to see how graphs change.

Use it for: Visualizing your algebra homework, understanding functions, and making sense of graphs.

Learn, Study & Practice

These sites have videos, lessons, and practice problems to help you learn and review.

CK-12

What it is: Think of this as a free online textbook, but way more interesting. It mixes readings with videos, examples, and interactive practice to help you learn.

Use it for: Getting a complete lesson on a topic from start to finish.

What it is: A free online curriculum that covers Pre-Algebra, Algebra 1, Algebra 2, and Geometry. It's organized like a class, with video lessons followed by practice problems.

For a Fun Challenge & Brain Boost

Try these sites to practice math in a more creative and fun way.

Brilliant

What it is: This site teaches you math through fun, interactive puzzles. It focuses on making you think like a problem-solver instead of just memorizing formulas.

Use it for: When you're bored with regular homework and want to challenge yourself.

Go to Brilliant →

FreeRice

What it is: A simple quiz game where you can practice your multiplication and basic math facts. For every question you get right, they donate rice to people in need.

Use it for: A quick, fun way to sharpen your basic skills while doing something good for the world.
