Graphing & Solving Tools

Use these sites to solve and graph equations. The Desmos calculator can be used on the SOL Exam.

Desmos Graphing Calculator

What it is: The best online graphing calculator, hands down. Just type in an equation and it shows you the graph instantly. You can plot points, make tables, and play with sliders to see how graphs change.

Use it for: Visualizing your algebra homework, understanding functions, and making sense of graphs.

Brilliant

What it is: This site teaches you math through fun, interactive puzzles. It focuses on making you think like a problem-solver instead of just memorizing formulas.

Use it for: When you're bored with regular homework and want to challenge yourself.
