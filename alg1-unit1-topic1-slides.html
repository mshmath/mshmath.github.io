Definitions: Expression vs. Equation

Expression

A math phrase containing numbers, variables, and operations.

3x + 5

NO EQUAL SIGN

Equation

A math sentence stating that two expressions are equal.

3x + 5 = 20

HAS EQUAL SIGN

Definitions: The Parts

5x + 2

Variable

A symbol (usually a letter) for an unknown value.
Ex: x, y, n

Constant

A number on its own that does not change.
Ex: 2, 5, -8

The "Turn-Around" Rule

Flip the Order!

If you see "Than" or "From", the number mentioned first goes last!

Example 1

"5 less than a number x"

x − 5

Wrong: 5 − x

Example 2

"10 subtracted from y"

y − 10

Grouping Symbols

Parentheses ( )

Use parentheses when an operation happens to a group.

"Twice the sum of x and 5"

2(x + 5)

"5 times the difference of y and 3"

5(y − 3)
