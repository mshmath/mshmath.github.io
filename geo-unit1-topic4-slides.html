Anatomy of a Venn Diagram

U (Universe)
A • x
• y (Not in A)

Set (A): A collection of objects (the circle).

Element (x): An object inside the set.

Universe (U): The box containing everything.

Complement (~A): Everything in the box but outside the circle (like point y).

Union vs. Intersection

Union (A ∪ B)

"Everything in A OR B"

(All shaded)

Intersection (A ∩ B)

"Everything in A AND B"

(Only the middle)
