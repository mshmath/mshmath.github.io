Part 1: Like Terms

Definition

Terms that have the same variable raised to the same exponent.

Like Terms

They match!

7x and 3x
5 and -2
4y² and -y²
NOT Like Terms

Different letter or power.

7x and 7y
x² and x
5a and 5

Identify Like Terms

Expression: 4x + 7 - 2x + 5

Find the x-terms:
4x and -2x
Find the numbers (Constants):
+7 and +5

Combining Like Terms

The 2 Steps:

  1. Add/Subtract Coefficients: Combine the numbers in front.
  2. Keep the Variable: The letter part stays EXACTLY the same.
3 apples + 2 apples = 5 apples
3x + 2x = 5x

Part 2: Distributive Property

The "Rainbow" Method

Multiply the term on the outside by EVERY term on the inside.

a(b + c) = ab + ac
Example
2(x + 4)

2 times x... and 2 times 4...

= 2x + 8

Putting It All Together

Order of Operations

Clear parentheses first!

6x + 2 + 4x

Step 2: Combine

Combine like terms.

10x + 2
