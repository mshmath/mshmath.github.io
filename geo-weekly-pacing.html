Monday: Conditional Statements & Truth Values

  • Focus: Intro to propositions, true/false statements.
  • Activities: Warm-up, interactive notes, example problems.

Tuesday: Converse, Inverse, Contrapositive

  • Focus: Transforming conditional statements.
  • Activities: Interactive notes, in-class practice.

Wednesday: Symbolic Logic

  • Focus: Translating statements into symbolic form.
  • Activities: Interactive notes, IXL/SOL practice.

Thursday: Venn Diagrams

  • Focus: Representing set relationships visually.
  • Activities: Interactive notes, practice, Unit 1 Quiz.

Unit 1 Essentials

Vocabulary: conditional, converse, inverse, contrapositive, negation, conjunction, disjunction, Venn diagram.

Monday: Parallel Lines & Transversals

  • Focus: Identifying angles formed by a transversal.
  • Activities: Warm-up, interactive notes, examples.

Tuesday: Angle Relationships

  • Focus: Alternate interior/exterior, corresponding angles.
  • Activities: Interactive notes, in-class practice.

Wednesday: Proving Lines Parallel

  • Focus: Using angle relationships to prove parallelism.
  • Activities: Interactive notes, IXL/SOL practice.

Thursday: Problem Solving

  • Focus: Applying angle relationships to solve problems.
  • Activities: Practice, Unit 2 Quiz.

Unit 2 Essentials

Vocabulary: transversal, alternate interior/exterior, corresponding, same-side interior, parallel.

Monday: Lines & Point Symmetry

  • Focus: Intro to types of symmetry.
  • Activities: Warm-up, interactive notes, examples.

Tuesday: Reflections and Rotations

  • Focus: Reflecting figures across lines and rotating around points.
  • Activities: Interactive notes, in-class practice.

Wednesday: Translations and Dilations

  • Focus: Sliding and resizing figures.
  • Activities: Interactive notes, IXL/SOL practice.

Thursday: Sequences of Transformations

  • Focus: Combining multiple transformations.
  • Activities: Practice, Unit 3 Quiz.

Unit 3 Essentials

Vocabulary: symmetry, reflection, rotation, translation, dilation, preimage, image.

Unit 4 Essentials

Vocabulary: congruent, triangle inequality, SSS, SAS, ASA, AAS, HL.

Unit 5 Essentials

Vocabulary: similar triangles, AA, trigonometry, sine, cosine, tangent, Pythagorean Theorem.

Unit 6 Essentials

Vocabulary: parallelogram, rhombus, trapezoid, sector, arc, inscribed angle, central angle, circle equation.

Unit 7 Essentials

Vocabulary: surface area, volume, cross-section, prism, pyramid, sphere, scaling, similarity ratio.

Monday - Thursday: Comprehensive Review

  • Focus: Review all units, targeted practice with released SOL test problems, Q&A sessions.
  • Goal: Intensive preparation for the Geometry Final exam.
