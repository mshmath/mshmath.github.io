Finding Your Why

A Personal Reflection on Your Journey Back to School
The Assignment
Welcome back. Taking the step to return to school is an incredibly brave decision. It takes courage to restart a journey, but you are here for a reason. Before we dive into the work, let's take a moment to understand that reason.
• What was the moment that made you say "It's time"?
• What is your drive? Who or what are you doing this for?
• What does your future look like after this program? (A new job, degree, certificate, friends, or just to learn?)
• How does it feel to take this first step?
• On the hard days, what thought will keep you moving forward?
Instructions: This is a "Free Write." Do not worry about spelling, grammar, punctuation, or sentence structure. Just let your thoughts flow onto the paper. This is for you to express yourself freely.
"It's never too late to be who you might have been."
