1. Which of the following sets of side lengths CANNOT form a triangle?

  1. 8, 11, 15
  2. 10, 10, 10

2. In \(\triangle ABC\), \(m\angle A = 100^\circ\), \(m\angle B = 30^\circ\), and \(m\angle C = 50^\circ\). Which is the longest side of the triangle?

  1. \(\overline{AB}\)
  2. \(\overline{BC}\)
  3. \(\overline{AC}\)
  4. Cannot be determined.

3. The measures of the angles in a triangle are \(2x\), \(2x\), and \(5x\). What is the measure of the largest angle?

  1. 20°
  2. 40°
  3. 90°
  4. 100°

4. An exterior angle of a triangle measures 125°. If one of the remote interior angles is 80°, what is the measure of the other remote interior angle?

  1. 45°
  2. 55°
  3. 80°
  4. 205°

5. Which postulate proves the two triangles are congruent?

  1. SSS

6. Given \(\triangle ABC \cong \triangle DEF\). If \(AB = 20\) and \(DE = 3x + 5\), what is the value of x?

  1. 5
  2. 15
  3. 25

7. You are given that \(\overline{AB} \cong \overline{XY}\) and \(\overline{BC} \cong \overline{YZ}\). What additional information is needed to prove \(\triangle ABC \cong \triangle XYZ\) by SSS?

  1. 5
  2. 7

  1. Yes, because 9 + 15 > 25.
  2. Yes, because 25 > 15.
  3. No, because 15 - 9 is not less than 25.
  4. No, because 9 + 15 is not greater than 25.
