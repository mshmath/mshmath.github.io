The Master Formula
$y = \mathbf{\color{#dc2626}{a}} \cdot f(x - \mathbf{\color{#2563eb}{h}}) + \mathbf{\color{#16a34a}{k}}$
a (Shape)
Stretch/Compress
Reflection
h (Horizontal)
Left (+) / Right (-)
OPPOSITE
k (Vertical)
Up (+) / Down (-)
EXACT
Shifts & Stretches
$+ k$ Shift UP
$- k$ Shift DOWN
$(x - h)$ Shift RIGHT
$(x + h)$ Shift LEFT
$|a| > 1$ Vertical Stretch (Skinny)
$0 < |a| < 1$ Vertical Compress (Wide)
Reflections & Rotations
$- f(x)$ Reflect X-Axis (Vertical Flip)
$f(-x)$ Reflect Y-Axis (Horizontal Flip)
180° Rot $(-x, -y)$
(Double Reflection)
90° CCW $(-y, x)$
(Swap & Negate 1st)
270° CCW $(y, -x)$
(Swap & Negate 2nd)
