Worked-Out Examples

Example 1: Evaluate

\( 27^{\frac{2}{3}} \)

\(\rightarrow (\sqrt[3]{27})^2\)

\(\rightarrow (3)^2\)

\(\rightarrow 9\)

Example 2: Rewrite

\( (\sqrt[5]{x})^3 \)

\(\rightarrow \text{Root is 5, Power is 3}\)

\(\rightarrow x^{\frac{3}{5}}\)
